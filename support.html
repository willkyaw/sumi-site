Common questions

Which countries does Sumi support?

Sumi currently covers Japan (民泊 / minpaku), France, and Korea, including night caps, licence steps, and — for Japan — many municipal ordinances. You pick your country during onboarding and can change it later in the Account tab.

Where is my data stored? Do you have my information?

Everything you enter stays on your device. Sumi has no servers and no accounts, so we never receive your data. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

How do I export or delete my data?

Open the Account tab. From there you can export your data as a file, delete your profile, or erase everything. Deleting the app from your iPhone also removes all stored data.

How does the “Ask Sumi” AI helper work?

It’s an optional chat that sends your question to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate an answer. It’s off unless you open it. If you never use it, nothing leaves your device.

Can I import my bookings?

Yes. In the Dates section you can paste an iCal calendar link from Airbnb or Booking.com, and Sumi will read your stay dates directly from that calendar.

Is Sumi legal advice?

No. Sumi offers general guidance to help you understand the rules, but requirements change and vary by location. Always confirm with your local authority before acting.
