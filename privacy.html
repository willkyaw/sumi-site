Privacy Policy

Effective date: 5 July 2026

The short version: Sumi has no servers and no accounts. Everything you enter stays on your iPhone. We don’t collect it, we can’t see it, and we don’t sell or share it. The only time any data leaves your device is if you choose to use the optional “Ask Sumi” AI helper.

Who we are

Sumi (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is an iOS app that helps short-term rental hosts in Japan, France, and Korea understand licensing rules and stay compliant. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information.

What we collect

We do not collect your personal data. Sumi has no backend servers, no user accounts, and no login. Information you enter into the app — such as your name, email, hosting goals, licence progress, document checklists, logged stays, and revenue notes — is stored locally on your device in the app’s private storage. It never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export or share it yourself.

No analytics. We don’t use any analytics, telemetry, or crash-reporting SDKs.

No tracking. We don’t track you across apps or websites, and we don’t use advertising identifiers.

No third-party sharing. Because we never receive your data, there is nothing for us to share or sell.

The optional “Ask Sumi” AI helper

Sumi includes an optional AI chat feature. It is turned off by default and only runs if you choose to use it.

  • When you send a message to Ask Sumi, that message — along with relevant context about your hosting situation that the app assembles to answer well — is sent to Anthropic, our AI provider, to generate a reply. This is subject to Anthropic’s Privacy Policy.
  • Your chat messages are used only to produce a response in the moment. Sumi does not store your conversations on any server we operate — we don’t operate one.
  • If you never open Ask Sumi, no data is ever transmitted off your device.
  • Please avoid entering sensitive personal information of your guests into the chat.

Calendar / booking import

If you choose to import your bookings, you paste an iCal calendar link (for example from Airbnb or Booking.com). The app fetches that calendar directly from the provider to read your stay dates. We do not receive or store a copy of it — the imported dates are saved only on your device.

Your data, your control

  • Export: You can export your data as a file at any time from the Account tab.
  • Delete: You can delete your profile or erase everything from within the app. Deleting the app from your device also removes all of its stored data.

Children

Sumi is intended for adults managing rental properties and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we’ll update this page and revise the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at willkyaw24@gmail.com, or see the Support page.

Sumi provides general guidance, not legal advice. Always confirm requirements with your local authority.
